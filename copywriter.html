Job Description

As a copywriter at MediaX, you will be working closely with client strategists & creative team to write clear and concise copy for ads, social media, and websites. You’ll be responsible for creative copies, captions, audio scripts & other textual elements that accompany the creatives. You should also be prepared to assist with other creative processes, such brainstorming of campaigns, visual briefs etc.

Roles & Responsibilities

May include but are not limited to:

  • Creating and editing original copy that is accurate, well-researched, and meets client/company needs and deadlines.

  • Assist the graphic team in creating content that resonates with the clients.

  • Assisting in other aspects of the creative or research processes to create cohesive, accurate content.

  • Edit and proofread copy as needed

  • Use SEO principles to maximize the copy’s reach.

  • Ideation and brainstorming for new business and contribution to business pitches

  • Conduct adequate research & gathering relevant material for interactive content development along with the design team

  • Competition Analysis

  • Monitor Digital & New Media industry trends and competitor offering s to identify potential business avenues and improve current offerings (better value for customer and better financial value for MediaX)
