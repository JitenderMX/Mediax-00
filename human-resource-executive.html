Job Description

As an HR executive at MediaX, your primary role will be to recruit, support and develop talent through developing policies and managing procedures. You will supervise administrative tasks and contribute to making the company a better place to work. We want you to be the best, to get the best out of our employees.

Roles & Responsibilities

May include but are not limited to:

  • Must manage end-to-end recruitment procedures like, understanding hiring requirements, creating and placing job advertisements, actively posting at job portals and professional networking sites.

  • Systematically lining up interviews & being a part of the rounds.

  • Conducting entry and exit of employees.

  • Creating offer, appraisal, termination & other required documents.

  • Managing the overall hiring process.

  • Must work closely with the leadership team to understand the hiring needs of the company.

  • Must be aware of the current employment industry standards

  • Must create performance reports (monthly/quarterly) and design incentive programmes to motivate employees.

  • Must keep records of leaves and overtime of every employee so the same can be reverted back at the time of bonuses.
